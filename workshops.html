workshops

design with us

We'd like to work with a group of people to help us design technology for a community garden. If you're interested and have some spare time, please come along. All you will need to do is: give us your views and thoughts, help come up with ideas, create prototypes, and provide critique.

We will be running two workshop. You may attend either or both of these workshops.

wednesday 13 december

In this workshop we want to explore what people might want from a community garden. For example: What sorts of things might attract you to go to a garden? What do you want to do while you are there? What sort of environment do you like? We will also have a look at what other people are doing with technology and get your views on that.

Then, we want to start coming up with ideas for the sorts of technology that might make your experience in a community garden better.

friday 15 december

In this workshop, our students will have created low fidelity prototypes (made of paper, cardboard, mock-ups) and will demonstrate these prototypes to everyone. We want your thoughts and feedback on those prototypes: What is good? What is not so good? How could they be improved? What issues might arise? This will help the students refine the design of the concepts.
