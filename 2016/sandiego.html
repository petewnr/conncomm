San Diego

San Diego is a city looking to the future. It was seen and recognized as a city of innovation and opportunity. A forward thinking city and noted for its liveability, business climate, educational institutions, leadership, demography and cultural diversity. Building on the existing infrastructures and getting all people involved in the decision the city was developed for them. The rise of this smart city is about engagement of the people and having them educated in the possibilities that are available – building on the education of talented innovators to create a city where talent, innovation and technology live together in a relaxed better lifestyle.

San Diego redefines what a smart city is by working for everybody and isn't daunted by the challenges of the future. They keep experimenting and trying till they find something that works . . . a SMART CITY.

Overview of that city

The city is a collaboration of resources from government, business, education and non-profit organisations driving existing programs to identify new opportunities, embracing existing collaborators to be a leading smart city. Its drive and use of resources has seen them implement initiatives that empowers consumers, improve environmental quality, drive economic growth and make San Diego an environmentally conscious clean city not reliant on fossil fuel. A city addressing the needs of the C21st.

Who is the target?

The people of San Diego are the winners in this story. The innovations by Cleantech- San Diego will make San Diego and the neighbouring counties smarter, cleaner and a more liveable city. The world inherently is a going to be a better place and live longer because Cleantech and the people of San Diego are making San Diego a better, liveable place for future generations.

What specifically makes San Diego smart?

San Diego is dedicated to make a city that is sustainable and more pleasant for all who live there. The main focus is to make it green and clean and a relaxing place to live. By using technology (ICT and IoT) they are changing the way the people of San Diego see their city and they want to be a part of its development. Having a say and being a beneficiary of what happens.

The city has recognised the education of people is important to making them a smart city and teaching young innovators has seen the development of many world breaking developments – bio fuel from algae, the development of the human gene through Illumina and the use of IoT technology to monitor the power usage and find new and clean ways of creating energy.

Example project

Smart buildings – Port

The Port of San Diego is a demonstration of smart technology committed to reducing the city’s carbon footprint. To do this The city uses smart sensors, big data, and predictive analytics as powerful tools as arsenal toward achieving our greenhouse gas reduction goals. The Port is installing solar panels on the roof of its primary cruise ship terminal, which is expected to offset approximately 70 percent of the building’s energy consumption. It is effectively a huge power source that feeds energy back into the city’s power grid without the use of carbon fuels.

Electric cars

San Diego's commitment to to have a greener cleaner city has seen the use of technology to tackle greenhouse emissions. Forty percent of greenhouse emissions come from transportation - cars, trucks and motorbikes. By using electric cars and electric car power stations San Diego has been able to reduce gas emissions and transition to a cleaner environment and energy independence. As one of the nation’s leading cities in adopting clean transportation, San Diego has one of the nation’s highest levels of electric vehicle drivers per capita – 21710 electric cars, 1092 public charging stations and 75 DC fast chargers.

City for the people

Developing run down areas of the city has given these areas life and encouraged people to come to these spaces that are now full of life and the heritage of San Diego has been restored. One such district is the Gaslamp Quarter - a district usually run down no inhabited (bad things happened and was dark) and has been transformed to preserve the beauty of the historical area. The development has flourished with restaurants, residencies and sporting facilities that bring people to this area that is now an important part of the city. These are world class facilities that draw in people from all over the world.

In changing the site, the plan was to make it different, vibrant and an exciting area that will make it a more liveable district. The goal being to increase population to the area and create more employment. Facilities that brought people to the area were:

  • New residential buildings and people brought there by a cultural hub - library
  • Sporting facilities and convention centres
  • Port area

Future Projects

The people of San Diego are the winners in this story. The innovations Clean tech San Diego are to make San Diego and the neighbouring county a smarter, cleaner and more liveable city. The world inherently is a going to be a better place and live longer because of the thought taken to make San Diego a better liveable place for years to come. The plan of the Cleantech smart city is to reduce the levels of greenhouse emissions by 10% by the year 2020 and a further 15% by 2025.

The forward direction of a clean city San Diego will start a Smart Water pilot program to make the city more drought resilient. Using IoT technologies San Diego will have a sustainable water management that will help the residents use water wisely and give a better quality of life.

Is it really making an impact on peoples lives?

The people of San Diego have shown great commitment to the development of the Smart city and taken ownership. Although there is funding from businesses the local people are able to be involved with fundraising for facilities - the library in the down town region. Although I have not been able to find statistical data that shows the improvement of within the city there is obvious development that makes regions more appealing and liveable.

Criticism

San Diego has presented a plan to make life better for it's residence and in doing so will be giving back to the earth by not taking and destroying Mother nature. This is always a positive if the earth is saved.

To make criticism of a city considered to be smart you need to look at the aspects they have tried to change. From the research I have looked at the key aspects needed to make change are: Energy, Energy, Finance, Mobility, ICY and cyber security and the General liveability of the city. San Diego has been able to address all of these aspects to make it a Smart City and a place where people want to visit and live.
