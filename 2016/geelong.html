Geelong: Advancing to become a Smart City of the future

Introduction

Geelong strongly believes that the way to be acknowledged and compete globally is through the advancement of the digital economy. This is due to the technological disruptions to industries which evidently has impacted the economic performance of the city. Their aim is to improve its urban services in order to increase its future employment opportunities, and in turn would help enhance a healthy and socially-connected community, and economic vibrancy. To assist in this advancement, smart campuses/universities will have a massive contribution to Geelong becoming a smart city "to drive growth, sustainability and inclusion for stronger and more resilient urban and regional economies".

Who are they prioritizing?

Currently, Geelong is focused on making their own university (Deakin University) a smart campus, this it through the application of the "three brains" framework; industrial brain dimension, customer-centric brain dimension, and meta brain dimension. Smart campus is a powerful source of momentum and innovation.

“Behind every smart city is a smart university” – Dr Simon Eassom, IBM

Current Project

Application of the “three brains” framework; industrial brain dimension, customer-centric brain dimension, and meta brain dimension.

Future Plans

Geelong is currently still in the process of planning their transformation into a smart city, however, they have come up with five success factors for pursuing a smart city; strong and sustained leadership, a robust architectural design and plan, a collaborative approach, underpinning infrastructure, and a customer centric approach.

Their end goal is to improve connectivity in the community, they believe that this can be achieved by enhancing the economic vibrancy and through smart campuses.

Who is engaging with the smart city?

Who got involved and to what extend?

Geelong's transformation to a smart city was proposed by the government, but the concept is being driven and executed by different organisations and its major sponsorship.
